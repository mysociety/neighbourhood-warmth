Home energy can be complicated, especially if you’re trying to figure it out alone.

Neighbourhood Warmth helps people to form teams around shared home energy challenges, such as these: [youtube video]

Each challenge is broken down into simple steps, with personalised advice.

The first challenge involves getting to know your home. Once completed you should have a clearer idea of:

  • home energy improvements you could make
  • ways to prioritise and plan these
  • how long they typically take
  • what they might cost

See for example: [youtube video]

We ask you to complete our challenges with a team of people who live near you, in houses similar to yours, because that way you’re more likely to share common problems and solutions. Plus, it’s more fun!
