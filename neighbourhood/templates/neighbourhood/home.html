It starts with understanding your home

Whether it’s a whole house assessment, thermal imaging, or something else – knowing more about your home and how you use it will help you make an informed decision on the right way forward for you and your household.

Neighbourhood Warmth guides you through the process via a series of simple steps, with personalised advice. By the end of the process, you’ll have a clearer idea of:

  • the home energy improvements you could make
  • the ways to prioritise and plan them
  • how long they typically take
  • and what they might cost
